Featured Dog Breeds

Golden Retriever

The origins of breed can be traced back to 1800s Scotland, where affluent Scots wanted a more skilled hunting dog. Golden Retrievers fit the bill with their above average intelligence, ability to navigate difficult terrain, and superb swimming skills. The Golden Retriever's friendly disposition makes a kind and cuddly addition to the family.

Cairn Terrier

Cairn Terriers trace their heritage back to the Isle of Skye off the coast of Scotland. The breed originally was grouped with the West Highland Terrier and Skye Terrier breeds, but the three breeds were reclassified as separate in the early 20th century. These feisty little dogs will make a wonderful family pet, but they may need some obedience training as puppies.

Labradoodle

The Labradoodle is cross breed combining a Standard Poodle with a Labrador Retriever. The breed first gained notoriety in Australia during the 1980s when a breeder demonstrated their ability to be effective guide dogs. Labradoodle's friendly personality, hypoallergenic coat, and high-activity level make them a great pet for an active family.
